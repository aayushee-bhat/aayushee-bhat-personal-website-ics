being a teaching assisstant

Over the last semester, I was the TA for PSY-2001 - Statistics and Research Methods I, under Professor Supriya Ray and the Teaching Fellow, Natania Narang. There was much learning in regards to the method of teaching itself. Condensing large amounts of information, and cherry-picking the ones that would be most useful is difficult. The challenge increases when students are at different levels of knowledge and understanding of such a subject. SRM I is the only course taken by Psychology students that incorporates a considerable amount of math and statistics, and often times there are many students who dropped math at various grades. So understanding the level and working up from there is something valuable I learnt, and very grateful for! I hope I was a supportive and understanding TA during my term <3
